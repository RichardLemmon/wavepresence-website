Why no cameras

Dignity first.

A parent aging at home. A family member recovering from surgery. A teenager staying alone for the weekend. People deserve to be looked after without being watched.

  • No cameras. No microphones. No video or audio, ever.
  • No photos of faces, rooms, or household activity leave the house.
  • Signals stay on the sensors. You see "Dad, Living Room" — never what Dad is doing.
  • Shareable only with the people you invite to the household.
